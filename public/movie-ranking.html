The Best Movies According To Sadhana

My Top 3 Movies Of All-Time.

Josee, the Tiger and the Fish

The genre of the movie is drama and romance. I really liked the good looking leads, especially the male lead Tsuneo. I enjoyed their initial meeting incident at the beginning and their interactions in the ending. There were many scenes which motivated me and some made my heart flutter.

Mission Impossible Series

The genre of the movie series is action and adventure. The way the spy completes his mission is very interesting. The stunts are breathtaking. Tom Cruise rocks in all the film series.

Geetha Govindam

The genre of the movie is drama and rom-com. It turned out to be very entertaining watching it. I loved the songs of this movie too.
